## Description: <br>
Use this skill for operating NVIDIA DOCA Management Service (DMS) on a BlueField, Arm host, x86 host, or K8s pod — bringing up the dmsd frontend and dmspe backend, picking deployment shape, choosing authentication mode, wiring gRPC client authorization, issuing gNMI Get/Set on YANG paths, gNMI Subscribe for streaming telemetry, gNOI for reboot/OS install/factory-reset/file transfer, or debugging frontend-vs-backend errors and logs. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>
